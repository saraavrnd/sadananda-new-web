Our Team

ROHIT DHAMANKAR

Rohit Dhamankar believes in the “Nada Yoga” as a path to discover oneself and immerse in absolute bliss He took to Carnatic music as an adult, and has formerly been trained in Hindustani music in his childhood. He was trained by Dr. Bhamathi Sudarshan in Austin. He has taken advanced lessons from Kalaimamani Charumathi Ramachandran, a doyenne of MLV baani. He also honours the Carnatic Trinity by helping organize Tyagaraja Aradhana and Mudduswami Dikshitar/Shyama Shastri days in Austin every year. Professionally, Rohit is involved in technologies that help prevent hackers from breaking into networks. He has worked at many start-ups in the Austin area. He has traveled for work to over 30 countries, and enjoys travel and interacting with different cultures.

DR. SRIVIDHYA RAMAMOORTHY

Dr. Srividhya, an Alumni of Barathi Womens college, Madras university at Anna university campus, moved to the US in 1997 in pursuit of higher education. Graduated with Ph.D in Microbiology from University of Montana, she has worked as a Faculty at Losrios Community College, Sacramento teaching Microbiology. Currently, she works for Sacramento Municipal Service Agency as a Laboratory Manager for a wastewater treatment plant.

Srividhya volunteers at children’s school for various programs and enjoys working with likeminded people to support society in whatever way possible.

She is a true believer of giving back to the society and doing the right thing

SRIKAANTH SANTHANAM

Srikaanth, a Chennai native and alumni of BITS Pilani, India & Carnegie Mellon University, USA, has been living in USA since 2007. He is currently working in the Financial Services industry as a Principal in a Crypto marketplace.

A strong believer in our culture, healthy body & mind, and giving back to the community, Srikaanth has been part of numerous local and global support organizations such as NYCares, iMentor, and similar organizations focusing on providing strong educational foundations, opportunities, and support.
