Educational Assistance

Intro for this section - 2 liners...

  • 270 students provided with free after school tuition classes. 30 professional teachers provide unparalleled education

  • World class amenities through smart classrooms, computers, digital display boards, etc.

  • Tremendous improvement in academic performance of the students

  • Scholarships provided for both school and college fees for deserving students

A childâ€™s environment plays a crucial role in shaping his/her future. Many children from low-income communities come from single-parent households or an abusive environment with alcoholic fathers. They are also, more often than not, first-generation learners.

On one hand, parents with little or no formal education are unable to help children academically. On the other hand, poor economic conditions act as a deterrent for deserving students who want to pursue higher education. Through our Education Assistance Programme we address both concerns.

In 2010, we started an evening tuition centre with 7 students at our premises to provide free, after school coaching & today, we have 270 students from classes 3-12. We work closely with government/municipal school teachers to identify deserving students. A team of 30 professional teachers including a few of our own elders impart unparalleled education to the students.

We also support first generation college going students by securing college seats & providing financial support for their college fees. We support around 60 students every year for their college education.
