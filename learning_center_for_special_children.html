Learning Center for Special Children

Intro for this section - 2 liners...

  • 47 students with learning difficulties provided with specialised education.

  • Students from poor economic backgrounds with autism, dyslexia, slow learning ability, low IQ, etc are chosen.

  • V-Excel educational trust which pioneers in special education is our technical partner

  • 9 trained teachers conduct classes everyday. occupational and physio therapy is provided to support their education.

Special children need special care. It is with this simple premise that we started the Anandam Learning Centre in 2018 for children with learning difficulties such as autism, dyslexia, slow learning disability, low IQ, etc.

Aimed for children whose families cannot afford specialised care, ALC was started as the 10th satellite branch of V-Excel Charitable Trust, an institution that pioneers in providing specialized education to children with learning difficulties. 47 of our students from the Anandam Tuition Centre were identified as having such learning difficulties & are currently in our Anandam Learning Centre for Special children.

Under the technical guidance of V-Excel, we provide physiotherapy, occupational therapy and develop the fine and gross motor skills of children through a range of activities & remedial classes. When counselled and guided, families are better equipped in adopting techniques and skills to help their children.

We are currently in the process of constructing a Free school for Special children coming from poor economic backgrounds with all amenities and infrastructure. To know more about this project click here.
